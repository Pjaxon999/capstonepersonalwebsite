About Peter Jackson:

Peter Jackson has led a life full of adventure and curiosity. Born under the foggy skies of the United Kingdom, Peter moved to the United States at the young age of 8, leaving behind the misty shores of his homeland. With a heart full of wonder and a free spirit, he faced the challenges of emigrating to a new country, navigating its complexities and opportunities with resilience.

In America, Peter grew up amidst the bustling world of stars and stripes, where he developed a deep thirst for knowledge. This passion for learning eventually led him to the University of Colorado at Boulder, where he earned not one, but two degrees—in Philosophy and Sociology. Peter distinguished himself amongst his colleagues exploring the depths of existential thought and complexities of social theory.

But Peter's quest for knowledge didn't stop there. Ever the curious learner, he's now venturing into the vast world of technology, learning the skills of web development. Armed with a keen intellect and a passion for coding, he's diving into the digital world. With his eyes set on the skills needed to build websites, he strives to develop the skills needed to flourish in the tech industry.

When he's not contemplating the mysteries of the universe, unraveling the hidden dynamics of society, or honing his coding skills, Peter enjoys battling opponents in the virtual world of video games, making music, riding his motorcycle, traveling, and spending time with the family dog. He's known for his sharp mind, hearty laugh, and loyalty to his friends and family.

A philosopher, gamer, musician, aspiring web developer, and a lifelong learner. He hopes for his journey to continue to be filled with exploration, growth, and new adventures!
